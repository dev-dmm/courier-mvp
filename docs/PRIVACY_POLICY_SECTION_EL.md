To improve delivery quality and protect our store from fraud, we participate in a

- **Order details (non-personal):**
  - Order number
  - Order amount
  - Payment method
  - Shipping method
  - Order status

   - Protection of stores from payment fraud
   - Reduction of failed deliveries

   - Analysis of failed delivery causes
